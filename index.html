Hello! I am a PhD student at the Department of Artificial Intelligence Convergence at Chonnam National University. I work under the supervision of Hyung-Jeong Yang. My research focuses on how AI can learn the aspects of human-human interaction when interacting with people. In addition, I am interested in researching and developing a model that recognizes human emotions through multimodality. Emotion recognition is basic research that can be expanded to study depression classification, social interaction, and empathy.
